ü•ò Pilau Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 kg beef
  • 2 onions, garlic & ginger
  • Pilau masala
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Oil, salt, and water

Instructions

  1. Fry onions till brown, add garlic and ginger.
  2. Add beef and cook till soft.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices.
  4. Mix in rice and water, let it cook slowly.

Tip: Tastes even better with a side of kachumbari!
